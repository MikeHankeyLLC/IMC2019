Alternative venue

The IMC venue is neighboured by a hotel at the same lake as the KiEZ Bollmannsruh. You can also book a room in this hotel, but booking is on your own. The IMC participation fee is then 90 EUR for the remaining expenses (lunch, dinner, conference facilities). See the Registration Form for details.

The walking distance from the hotel to the IMC venue is about 600 metres. Both locations have a decent number of parking lots for cars. You can book a room at the Hotal Boolmannsruh online.

The hotel provides accommodation in three separate residences for 130 guests in 58 rooms: single rooms, double rooms, a family room and apartments with a terrace. Every room is equipped with cable TV with flat screen, internet connection, telephone, bathroom with toilet, and a shower.

The large restaurant has a capacity of around 80 guests plus a terrace overlooking the riding arena.

Alternative Accommodations

On the registration form and the payment page, you will also find a “no accommodation” option. While we generally recommend the other options which include lodging at the conference venue, we nevertheless offer you the possibility to book your own accommodation. This might be useful, for example, if you insist on single accommodation when we are out of the very limited number of singles available, or if you take your spouse or friend with you, and you want him/her to be completely independent of the IMC schedule. If you choose the no-accommodation option, however, you are responsible for finding suitable alternative accommodation. These are some links to the websites of nearby hotels and boarding houses you may want to look at in this case:

  • Hotel*** Vinársky Dom (2km from the LOC)
  • Hotel Tilia (2.5km from the LOC)

Transportation between the conference venue and these or other alternative accommodations are also the responsibility of the participant choosing the “no-accommodation” option. However, a taxi ride will typically cost no more than 3-5€.

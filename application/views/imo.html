IMO Membership

As a participant of the IMC 2019 in Germany, you may also be interested in becoming a member of the International Meteor Organization (IMO). Members receive the bimonthly journal WGN (on paper or electronically, or both) and have free digital access not only to the WGN archive but also to the Handbook for Meteor Observers and the Meteor Shower Workbook to two invaluable tools for observing meteors.

More information about the activities of the IMO can be found on the website.

The standard membership fee currently amounts to 26 € (21€ for an electronic-only subscription to WGN).

If you become a member at the IMC in Bollmannsruh, we give you a reduction of 5 € on the membership fee for your first year. We also reward current members participating at the IMC with this reduction of 5 € on the membership fee for their next year’s membership provided they renew at the IMC.

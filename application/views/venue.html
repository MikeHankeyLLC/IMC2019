Conference Venue

The conference will be held at the KiEZ Bollmannsruh which is situated 60km from Berlin.

Alternative venue

The IMC venue is neighboured by a hotel at the same lake as the KiEZ Bollmannsruh. You can also book a room in the hotel, but booking is on your own. The IMC participation fee is then 90 EUR for the remaining expenses (lunch, dinner, conference facilities).

The walking distance from the hotel to the IMC venue is about 600 metres. Both locations have a decent number of parking lots for cars. You can book a room at the Hotal Boolmannsruh online.
